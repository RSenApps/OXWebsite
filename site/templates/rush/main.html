Theta Chi is looking for quality individuals to join our ranks. Stop by some of our events or drop in at the house for a quick hello to see what we are all about. We look forward to getting to know you! Get pumped.

We at Theta Chi believe that tradition is meant to evolve. The fraternity experience should not be about antiquated rituals, but rather about making long-lasting friendships and helping each other fulfill our ambitions.

We’re proud of our individual and house accomplishments that make this house the wonderful community that it is. Theta Chis can be found in the Executive Board of the Interfraternity Council, on the Formula SAE race team, in the ranks the MIT ROTC program, in the MIT Crew boathouse, and even among the editors of the Tech. As IFC’s Most Improved for the 2014 Chapter of the Year, Theta Chis further show that they are committed to excellence. We believe a strong and cohesive living community is essential for personal growth and provides an invaluable opportunity to discover new passions and perspectives.

The brothers of Theta Chi recognize that a rewarding college experience involves a vibrant social life. We distinguish ourselves by dropping some of the jankiest beats in the college party scene and hosting some of the most creatively themed social events. With brothers and friends hailing from all over campus, our house facilitates connections between seemingly disparate social circles within (and even outside) MIT. We’re looking for guys who strive to be the best version of themselves they can be, whether that involves going on to create new gadgets at Apple, funding your own multimillion startup, or developing your skills in the jazz guitar. We look forward to meeting you.
